You wake up in...

You hurriedly jump out of your bed and get under the desk which is

just over a meter away. While under it, you hear a few things fall from it

including a glass of water you had left on the desk. The glass breaks

from the fall, and now the floor in front of you is littered with glasses

What do you do?

Shout for your family to come to help you.

Carefully go over the glasses and check on your family.
