You wake up in...

The stairs seem much safer to you, so you choose them, instead of

the elevator. As you go down the stair, you meet your neighbours, who

tell you that they too woke up because of the earthquake. You go

outside and see a lot of people on the street, horrified and sorrowful,

with many children crying.

What do you do?

Call your neighbors and friends to see if they're safe.

Go to the nearby park, open places are usually safer.
